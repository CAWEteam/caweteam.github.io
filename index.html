About CAWE

Center for Animal Welfare Economics (CAWE) is a nonprofit dedicated repository of economic research on animal welfare.

We translate complex research — where animal welfare meets economics — into plain language, gathered in one place.

Our mission is to show producers that adopting humane treatment can drive their bottom line, to help policymakers build smarter animal welfare legislation, and to support animal sanctuaries and nonprofit operators in building economically sustainable organizations.
